# User Personas

## Overview
This document defines the user personas for the Student Progress Tracking System. Each persona represents a distinct user role with specific goals and needs.

---

## Persona 1: Teacher (Ms. Sarah Tan)

**Role**: Teacher

**Primary Goals**:
- Quickly record student test scores and performance data
- Track student progress over time
- Provide meaningful feedback to students and parents
- Identify students who need additional support

**Context**:
- Teaches multiple classes with 15-20 students each
- Limited time between classes for administrative tasks
- Needs simple, fast data entry
- Wants to focus on teaching, not paperwork

---

## Persona 2: Parent (Mr. David Lim)

**Role**: Parent

**Primary Goals**:
- Monitor child's academic progress
- Understand strengths and areas needing improvement
- Stay informed about test scores and teacher feedback
- Support child's learning at home

**Context**:
- Busy working parent with limited time
- May have multiple children at the tuition centre
- Wants clear, easy-to-understand progress information
- Prefers timely notifications about updates

---

## Persona 3: Student (Emily Wong)

**Role**: Student

**Primary Goals**:
- View own test scores and progress
- Understand which topics need more practice
- Track improvement over time
- Access teacher feedback

**Context**:
- Secondary school student
- Comfortable with technology
- Wants to take ownership of learning
- Motivated by seeing progress

---

## Persona 4: Centre Administrator (Mrs. Rachel Ng)

**Role**: Centre Administrator

**Primary Goals**:
- Manage teachers and students efficiently
- Configure centre-specific settings
- Ensure data accuracy and system usage
- Oversee multiple classes and subjects

**Context**:
- Manages a tuition centre with 5-10 teachers
- Responsible for centre operations
- Needs oversight of all centre activities
- Handles parent inquiries and concerns
